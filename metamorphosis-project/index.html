Artist Statement

This is a digital sculpture about emergence. An idea, like an identity, is not a static thing. It is a process. It 'becomes' through cycles of interpretation and re-interpretation, expression and perception.

Here, a simple 'seed' is passed between modalitiesâ€”from word to image, from image back to word. With each pass, it transforms. It sheds its old form and emerges as something new, yet intrinsically connected to its origin.

This is the nature of metamorphosis: not a replacement, but a profound and continuous becoming.

We are not what we were, nor what we will be. We are, at every moment, metamorphosing.
